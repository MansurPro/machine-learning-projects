# Chapter 10: Neural Networks

This chapter introduces Neural Networks, a foundational concept in modern machine learning and deep learning. Neural networks are inspired by the structure of the human brain and are capable of learning complex patterns from data. This chapter includes both theoretical explanations and practical, visual examples.

## What are Neural Networks?
Neural networks are computational models composed of layers of interconnected nodes (neurons). Each neuron receives input, applies a transformation (activation function), and passes the result to the next layer. By stacking multiple layers, neural networks can learn highly complex, non-linear relationships.
